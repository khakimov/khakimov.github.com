[Death] Other Bad Aspects of Death

If you did know how much time left to live, how would you act differently from what you’re doing now? Would it focus your attention on making sure you did the things that were most important to you?

To find the most valuable thing in life ask the question what would you choose to do if you knew you had five years, ten years, what have you?

The possibility of death is ubiquitous.
