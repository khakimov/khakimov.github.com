About Marx and his teacher Hegel.

Hegel is the most important philosopher in up until his time.

The only Thought which Philosophy brings with it to the contemplation of History, is the simple conception of Reason; that Reason is the Sovereign of the World; that the history of the world, therefore, presents us with a rational process. Hegel, Lecture on the Philosophy of History

The only place where you can find the truth is in the world, in history.

Reason is the substance of the Universe; viz. that by wchi and in which all reality has its being and subsistence. On the other hand, it is the Infinite Energy of the Universe…

Modernity for Hegel is the period in which we understand that the only place we should look for truth is right around us, not in some divine (божественный) or ideal realm.

In traversing the past we have only to do with what is present… The life of the ever present Spitir is a circle of progressive embodiments, which looked at in one aspect still exist beside each other, and only looked at from another point of view appear as past.

Dialectical processes are how the past is retained in the present.

Thesis | Antithesis => Synthesis

Fathers | Sons => Synthesis or opposit of it - Negation

Conflict makes the world go round. History is reasonable. Truth is in the flesh of history, Hegel takes it from being the flesh of Jesus, to being the stuff of history. That’s the only realm where truth is found. So you can’t isolate youself and try to find the truth, truth is on the battlefield, in politics, on the streets, the truth will emerge in history. That is Hegel’s strong belieive.

The history of the world is none other than the progress of the consciousness of freedom; a progress whose development according to the necessity of its nature it is our business to investigate. Hegel

The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles. Communist Manifesto

On the basis of political economy itself, in its own words, we have shown that the worker sinks to the level of a commodity and becomes indeed the most wretched (несчастный) of commodities; that the wretchedness of the worker is in inverse proportion to the power and magnitude of his production… Marx

That creates self-estrangement or alienation. Create more and more, but get less and less, because prices go down and that’s the problem for workers.

The alienation of the worker in his product means not only that his labor becomes an object, an external existence, but that it exists outside him, independently, as something alien to him, and that it becomes a power on its own confronting him. It means that the life which he has conferred on the object confronts him as something hostile and alien.

Through your work you see more about yourself, you learn about yourself, from the work you do.
