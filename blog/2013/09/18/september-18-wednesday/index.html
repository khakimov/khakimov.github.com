For Freud and Civilization’s of discontents guilt is a modern problem. As we have modernization, as we have a more and more sophisticated society we have a greater and greater chance of the explosion of unsatisfied.

Freud is no friend of anarchism or hypermorality, no friend of socialism, because he thinks all of these are introducing new ideals that will be very difficult to live by.

Thus I have not the courage to rise up before my fellow-men as a prophet, and I bow to their reproach that I can offer them no consolation: for at bottom that is what they are demanding - the wildest revolutionaries no less passionately than the most virtuous believers. Civilization and Its Discontents

Freud wants to escape the pursuit of meaning through ideals. He doesn’t want us to strive to live on a plain that is divorced from our animal natures, divorced from instinct. He doesn’t want us to try to find a mode of consolation that obscures who we are. Because if we obscure who we are, then we create another source of suffering.

There is no perfect life for Freud, because there’s no one good way of living. But we can figure out how to make ourselves suffer less and that would be a significant achievement. All our desires are full of conflict.

Building Pyramids

Armies, police forces, courts and prisons are ceaselessly at work forcing people to act in accordance with the imagined order. A single priest often does the work of 100 soldiers far more cheaply and effectively. The priest convinces you to really believe this story, and then you don’t need all the soldiers and police forces to keep you in order. And this was the key for really creating stable social orders.

How exactly do you cause people to really believe in an imagined order, such as Christianity, Democracy or Capitalism?

Three main factors that prevent people from realizing that the order which organizes their lives and their society exists only in the imagination. We try to link it with the actual material reality which surrounds us.

Many people believe that every human being is an individual whose worth, whose true value does not depent on his or her position in the social hierarchy and does not depend on what other people think about you. The main idea of individualism is that each one os us, have within ourselves some kind of brillian ray of light that gives value and meaning to our lives.

The imagined order also shapes our deepest desires and wishes. Most people do not wish to accept the fact that the order governing their lives is imaginary, because it’s the imginary order that control and shapes the most fundamental wishes of the people.

Example: Many people today dream about going on vacation to some other country. But there is nothing natural or obvious about this desire. People today spend a great deal of time and effort and money on vacation abroad, because they have programmed by the main stories of their societies to want such things. People today are true believers in the stories of what scholars call the romantic consumerism.

Romantic consumerism is one of the most powerful stories today in the world, is a mesh, is a combination between two of the dominant late modern ideologies: romanticism and consumerism.

Romanticism tells us that in order to make the most of your life, of your human potential, you must have as many different experiences as you can. You must open yourself to a wide spectrum of emotions. You must sample various kinds of relationships. You must try all kinds of different foods. The best way of doing it is to break free from your daily routine, leave behind your familiar neighborhood and city, and go travel in distant land.

Consumerism tells us that in order to be happy - you must consume as many products and sevices as possible. If you feel what something is missing in your life, then you probably need to buy a new product, like a new car or new clothe. Or you need to buy some kind of service, yoga class or psychologie retreat, but you need to buy something to be really happy.

Differenece between realities objective, subjective and inter-subjective.

Objective - something that exists independently of human consciousness and of what human believe and invent and tell one another. Example: radio activity - is not a story that people invented and tell one another, existed and influenced people long before anybody knew about it.

Subjective - is something that exists depending on the consciosness and beliefs of a single individual and it will disappear or change if that particular individual changes his or her beliefs. Exmaple: many children believe in the existence of imaginery friend that only the child himself can see and can talk to. And usually as a child grows up, he stops believing in this imaginary friend.

Inter-subjective - is something that exists within the communication network that link the subjective consciosness of many individuals. If a single individual changes his/her beliefs or even dies, it is of little importance. However, if most individuals in the network die or change their beliefs, then the inter-subjective phenomenon will mutate and change or completely disappear. Example: Gods, nations, human rights, money, corporations and so on.
